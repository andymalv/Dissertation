		The wearisome grand passions and distasteful excitements of active lives, stressed to breaking point, are supplanted in the end by the implacable lassitude of walking: just walking. Serenity is the immense sweetness of no longer expecting anything, just walking, just moving on.  \par
		\hfill{Fr\`ed\`eric Gros, \textit{A Philosophy of Walking}}
